# 🏠 Airbnb Market Analysis Dashboard (Tableau)
## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes Airbnb listings data to uncover trends in property listings, pricing, room types, and revenue generation across different zip codes.

Using Tableau, I created an interactive dashboard that helps stakeholders understand:

* Airbnb property distribution
* Popular room types
* Pricing trends
* Revenue performance over time
* Geographic pricing differences

The dashboard transforms raw Airbnb listing data into actionable business insights that can support investment decisions, pricing strategies, and market analysis.

## 🎯 Business Problem

Airbnb hosts, property investors, and market analysts need to understand:

* Which property types dominate the market
* How pricing varies by location
* The relationship between bedrooms and listing prices
* Revenue trends throughout the year

Without proper analysis, pricing decisions may be inaccurate and investment opportunities may be overlooked.

This dashboard aims to provide a data-driven view of the Airbnb market.

## 📈 Key Dashboard Components
The dashboard components are:

* Distribution of listings by bedroom count
* Popularity of different room types
* Average price by number of bedrooms
* Revenue trends throughout the year
* Price variations across zip codes

## 🔍 Key Insights
**Market Composition**
* One-bedroom listings dominate the market.
* Entire-home rentals are the preferred accommodation type.

**Pricing Behavior**
* Listing prices increase with bedroom count.
* Premium zip codes command significantly higher prices.

**Revenue Performance**
* Revenue maintained an upward trajectory throughout the year.
* Seasonal demand patterns are present but growth remains strong.

**Geographic Trends**
* Certain neighborhoods offer stronger revenue potential due to higher average prices.

##💡 Recommendations
**For Airbnb Hosts**
* Consider investing in entire-home listings where feasible.
* Optimize pricing based on neighborhood benchmarks.
* Larger properties can generate higher revenue through premium pricing.

**For Property Investors**
* Focus on high-value zip codes with stronger pricing power.
* Analyze demand trends before purchasing investment properties.
* Evaluate bedroom count as a major revenue driver.

**For Business Analysts**
* Monitor seasonal demand fluctuations.
* Build predictive models for occupancy and revenue forecasting.
* Track pricing changes by location over time.
